Famous Monuments of India

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, symbolizing love and beauty.

Qutub Minar

The world’s tallest brick minaret, built in the 12th century in Delhi.

India Gate

A war memorial located in New Delhi dedicated to the soldiers of World War I.

Mysore Palace

An architectural marvel in Karnataka, known for its royal heritage and design.
